Picturing Urban Renewal

This site provides access to draft online material in support of the NEH Digital Projects for the Public Prototype Phase “Picturing Urban Renewal” grant application. This material is intended to accompany the written grant proposal and design document; further information about these mockups can be found in those documents.

Please note, although these mockups may appear resolved they are a rough draft vision for the website. The scrolling story served us as a dynamic visualization tool and is not fully functional. With funding from a Prototyping grant, we plan to user test, revise, and refine these design ideas and employ production-level technologies. (Modern browser required — e.g. not IE 11)
